What is RevPAR?

Revenue Per Available Room — the simplest measure of how hard your rooms are working. It combines your nightly rate and how often rooms are filled into a single number.

RevPAR = Nightly Rate × Occupancy %

A room at $300/night at 85% occupancy has a RevPAR of $255. A room at $80/night at 60% occupancy has a RevPAR of $48. Same room. Very different outcomes.
