What is RevPAR?

Revenue Per Available Room — the simplest measure of how hard your rooms are working. It combines your nightly rate and how often your rooms are filled into a single number.

RevPAR = Nightly Rate × Occupancy %

A room priced at $300/night at 85% occupancy has a RevPAR of $255. A room priced at $80/night at 60% occupancy has a RevPAR of $48. Same room. Very different outcomes.

STR Exposure — Unmanaged

HPD records confirm both buildings are registered as Class A — zero transient (Class B) units. Sub-30-day rentals carry fines of up to $5,000 per violation under Local Law 18. The wind-down is not optional. But it does not have to cost revenue — managed correctly, it is a revenue-positive transition.
